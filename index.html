About

Yitzhak Alvarez is a hard-working and dedicated student, and a leader who strives for success and loves to try out new things. He is an aspiring web developer and software engineer. He is currently a senior attending at the State University of New York at New Paltz in pursuit of a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science. During his spare time, Yitzhak is involved in clubs to help him reach his goals. He is an active member of the National Society of Black Engineers (NSBE), a nonprofit organization, and the Assistant Director of Videography for his school's tv station, WNPC-TV. Yitzhak interned for One EPIC Place in New Paltz as a Full Stack Engineer, where he created a room web-based scheduler.
